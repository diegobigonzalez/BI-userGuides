üìä What is Top of the House?

Top of the House provides a consolidated view of your organization's entire FX exposure. This view aggregates all FX positions at the corporate level, allowing you to understand the net currency risk after considering natural offsets between entities.

üí° Key Concept: The "Top of the House" view shows the net exposure resulting from algebraically summing positions across all group entities, reflecting the actual risk faced by the organization as a whole.

‚öôÔ∏è Policy Parameter Setup

The Policy Parameter Setup panel allows you to define differentiated hedging rules by currency pair tiers. Access it by clicking the settings icon.

Tier System

You can configure up to 3 policy levels with different parameters:

Tier 1 - Primary Pairs

Configure your most important currency pairs with specific thresholds and hedge ratios. Typically includes pairs like EURUSD, EURGBP, EURDKK with more aggressive policies.

Tier 2 - Secondary Pairs

Define rules for pairs with lower volume or intermediate priority. May include pairs like GBPUSD, USDCHF with higher thresholds.

Tier 3 - Other Pairs

Configure policies for remaining currency pairs with lower exposure. These typically have higher thresholds before recommending hedges.

Parameters per Tier

Parameter Description Example
Currency pairs Selection of pairs assigned to this tier EURUSD, EURGBP, EURDKK
Threshold Minimum exposure amount to generate a recommendation 23,250,000
Hedging policy % Percentage of exposure to hedge 85.00%
‚ö†Ô∏è Important: Changes to policy configuration immediately affect the recommendations shown. Ensure parameters reflect the approved hedging strategy.
